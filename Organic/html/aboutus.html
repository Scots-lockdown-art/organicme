OUR HISTORY

Opened in Kelso in 1992 we have been growing, importing and selling organic produce for now 30 decades. Originally a small company focusing on our organic bee products over the years we have continually expanded the business including opening our very own organic cafe just outside the farm.

We scoure the globe to import the best organic products we can to sell alongside our own local produce. We are now taking our business to the next level with our online shopping. We also promote our beautiful countryside in Kelso and this includes welcoming anyone to join our gardining course which takes place next to our farm!


Sign up for our Gardening Course!


OUR CAFE

Opened in 2004 our cafe gives our customers a nice space to relax with a warm organic coffee and cake or even a small lunch from our varied menu.

Whether you have spent the day exploring the local area, doing a bit shopping or just want to catch up with some friends we welcome you with big organic smiles!


OPENING TIMES:
Mon to Sat - 8am to 4pm


OUR BEES

The company originally opened to sell the honey we produced from our local bees. We expanded the product line to include other bee products such as beeswax, pollen, propolis, royal jelly and bee venom. Though the company has grown signifcantly we still put as much enthusiasm and focus into our bee farming and recommend you try our products which also helps support the surivival and growth of these essential insects and our bee farming.

Buy our Honey currently 2 for 1!
