OUR HISTORY

Opened in Kelso in 1992 we have been growing, importing and selling organic product for now 30 decades. Originally a small company focusing on our organic bee products over the years we have continually expanded the business. Purcashing more farm land and cows giving us organic dairy products, then in 2004 opening our very own organic cafe just outside the farm. We scoure the globe to import the best organic products we can to sell alongside our own local produce. We are now taking our business to the next level with our online shopping. We also try to promote the beaufitul countryside of our local Kelso area and this includes welcoming anyone to join our gardining course which takes place next to our farm!

OUR CAFE

Opened in 2004 our cafe gives our customers a nice space to relax with a warm organic coffee and cake or even a small lunch from our varied menu. OUR BEES

The company originaly opened to sell our honey we produced from our local bees. We expanded the product line to include other bee products such as beeswax, pollen, propolin, royal jelly and bee venom. Though the company has grown signifcantly we still put as much enthusiam and focus into our bee farming and recommend try our products which also helps support the surivival and growth of these essential insects and our farming
