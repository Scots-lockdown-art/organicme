GARDENING COURSE

SIGN UP


Sign up for more information or for any questions including advice on local accommodation get in touch via our Contact Us Page!

Running since 2018 we have several gardening courses starting from Spring until the end of Summer. They start 9am and end 4pm with one hour break for lunch. It is a great opportunity to experience the outdoors and connect with nature.

We have different levels from beginner to more advanced as well as more casual family and child courses. Not only will you have a delightful summer outdoors in our stunning farm area you will learn amazing new gardening skills you can take back home to work on your own garden.
